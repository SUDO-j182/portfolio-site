Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Jai, an IT enthusiast with a relentless drive to transition into the technology sector. Diagnosed with ADHD at the age of 8, I struggled in traditional school and work environments. However, over time, I’ve learned to turn ADHD into my greatest strength—allowing me to hyperfocus on complex technical subjects, break them down, and master them through persistence and hands-on problem-solving.

My passion lies in web development, cybersecurity, and software engineering, where I don’t just learn but strive to build custom solutions to real-world problems.

My Transition into Tech

For years, I worked in warehouse operations, where I developed skills in efficiency, quality control, and teamwork. While I excelled in practical problem-solving, I knew my real passion was in technology and computing. Determined to take control of my future, I immersed myself in IT skills, web development, and cybersecurity, training myself through structured learning and hands-on projects. I began with HTML and CSS, building small projects like this portfolio website and my CV. As I gained confidence, I expanded into JavaScript, creating practical tools such as: A Secure Random Password Generator – My first functional JavaScript project, which deepened my understanding of security and programming logic. A Custom Pixel Art Drawing Tool – Designed for favicon and pixel art creation, this project challenged me to manipulate the DOM, handle user input, and refine JavaScript interactivity. These projects allowed me to go beyond surface-level coding, pushing me to understand the underlying logic, optimize functionality, and create tailored solutions rather than simply replicating existing ones. Beyond coding, I developed a deep interest in cybersecurity, leading me to pursue multiple certifications and explore areas such as network security, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence.
