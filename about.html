Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Jai, I am an IT enthusiast with a strong determination to transition into the technology sector. Coming from a warehouse operations background, I have spent the last few years self-teaching, upskilling, and building projects to develop a solid foundation in web development, cybersecurity, and software engineering. My journey into tech is fueled by a passion for learning, problem-solving, and creating practical solutions.

My Transition into Tech

For years, I worked in warehouse operations, ensuring efficiency, quality control, and team collaboration. However, I have always been drawn to technology and computing, and I decided to take control of my future by immersing myself in IT skills, web development, and cybersecurity.

I started with HTML and CSS, creating simple projects such as this portfolio website and my CV. As I grew more confident, I introduced JavaScript, building functional tools like my secure random password generator and a simple drawing tool for creating favicons and pixel art. These projects allowed me to explore programming logic, the Document Object Model (DOM), and JavaScript interactivity.

Beyond coding, I have taken a strong interest in cybersecurity, earning multiple certifications and learning about network security, cloud computing, and AI through structured courses.

What I Have Done

  • Portfolio Website – Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, continuously improved to reflect my evolving skills.
