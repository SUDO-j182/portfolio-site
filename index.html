Who Am I?

I'm Jai — a self-taught developer transitioning into tech from a background in warehousing. Diagnosed with ADHD at age 8, I struggled in traditional learning environments, but now recognize it as a strength: a unique lens that drives my focus, resilience, and creative thinking. What started as a passion project — building a simple HTML/CSS portfolio — grew into a journey of continuous learning in web development, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. I bring with me a practical mindset, a love for solving problems, and a desire to build technology that works for people.

From Warehouse to Web Development

My early work history taught me discipline, teamwork, and efficiency — all qualities I now apply to programming.
