Welcome to My Portfolio

Hello and welcome to my site. I am a seven-year warehouse veteran transitioning into the tech industry. I designed this site to showcase my passion for programming, software development, web design, AI, and cybersecurity.

About Me

I was diagnosed with ADHD at eight years old, which made school incredibly difficult for me. I remember constantly questioning my teachers and their ability to offer meaningful advice about success. Eventually, I was expelled and only returned to take my exams, leaving with nothing but a single poor grade.

After school, I briefly attended college, earning a Level 2 IT User Diploma. From the age of 17 until late 2024, I worked in warehouses and factories. Due to ADHD and the social challenges it has presented in adulthood, I struggled to hold down a job for long periods. However, I’ve learned to harness my ADHD alongside my interests. I have an almost obsessive ability to focus on developing the skills needed to bring my passions to life—leading to projects like this website and many more.
