My Transition into Tech

After years working in warehouse roles focused on efficiency, quality control, and teamwork, I chose to take control of my future by diving headfirst into tech. I also explored cybersecurity, cloud computing, and AI through formal learning and certifications.

TempleOS Inspiration

I admire TempleOS for its minimalism, 16-color palette, and blend of raw vision with deep technical knowledge. The work of Terry A. Davis — someone who created an entire operating system alone — deeply resonates with me. Despite the challenges he faced, Terry channeled isolation into creation, leaving behind a system that was brilliant, artistic, and truly original.

His story reminds me that real passion isn’t about conformity — it’s about vision. And that’s the same mindset I bring to my projects.
