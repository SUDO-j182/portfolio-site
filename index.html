Welcome to My Portfolio

Hello! I'm Jai, an IT enthusiast passionate about software and web development, with a keen interest in AI. This portfolio showcases my journey into tech, my projects, and the skills I've acquired. Explore my work, learn about my inspirations, and connect with me for potential collaborations.

About Me

Diagnosed with ADHD at the age of 8, I found it difficult to settle in school. I left with only a GCSE grade D in English, as I struggled to concentrate in most lessons except for history and IT. Despite my interest, my attention span was insufficient, leading to my expulsion. I attended college briefly, earning a level 2 diploma in IT, and then worked in warehouses and factories from the age of 17. Due to social difficulties, I couldn't hold a job for long.

After years of dead-end jobs, I decided to leverage AI to teach myself industry-recognized skills and break into the IT industry. I am passionate about this journey and believe that earning a living doing what I love will help me finally settle down.
